Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Love Decoder (“the app”, “we”) is published by Gameplay Labs LLC. The app has no account, no login, and no server of our own. This policy explains how data is handled.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers. Your test answers, your saved type, and your selections stay only on your device.

Advertising

The app shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain data, including a device advertising identifier, IP address, and ad-interaction data. For users Google identifies as under 18, Google disables ads personalization. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings. See Google’s “How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services” and the Google Privacy Policy.

Children

The app is intended for users 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your choices

Because we store nothing on our side, you can clear all app data at any time by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling it.

Entertainment notice

Personality and compatibility content is for entertainment purposes only and is not psychological, medical, or relationship advice. Not affiliated with or endorsed by The Myers & Briggs Foundation or NERIS Analytics Limited.
