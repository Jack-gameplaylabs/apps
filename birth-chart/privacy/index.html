Birth Chart

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Birth Chart (“the app”, “we”) is published by Gameplay Labs LLC. The app has no account, no login, and no server of our own. This policy explains how data is handled.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers. Your birth date, birth time, birth place, and any charts or readings generated from them stay only on your device. All astrological calculations are performed on your device. Data processed by Google for advertising and analytics is described below.

Advertising

The app shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain data, including a device advertising identifier, IP address (from which approximate location may be derived), ad-interaction data, and diagnostic information such as app performance data. For users Google identifies as under 18, Google disables ads personalization. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings. See Google’s “How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services” and the Google Privacy Policy.

Analytics

The app uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the app is used (for example, which screens are opened and how often the app is launched) and to improve the app. Google processes this data on our behalf as our service provider, including app-interaction events, an app-instance identifier, coarse device information (such as device model and operating system version), approximate region derived from IP address, and limited app-level attributes such as your zodiac sun sign — but never your exact birth date, birth time, birth place, or the content of your charts and readings. This analytics data is not used to personalize ads and is not shared for advertising purposes. Analytics data is retained by Google for a limited period and then deleted. See the Google Privacy Policy for details.

Your advertising choices

Where required, the app shows a consent message that lets you choose whether ads are personalized. You can change your choice at any time in the app’s settings (“Ad privacy settings”). On iOS, the app also asks for tracking permission through Apple’s system prompt; you can change this later in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Your U.S. state privacy rights

Depending on where you live (for example, California), you may have the right to know, delete, or opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell personal information for money. Under some state laws, showing personalized ads may count as "sharing" or "selling"; you can opt out at any time through “Ad privacy settings” in the app’s settings (this is our “Your Privacy Choices” — Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information — control) or by resetting or limiting your advertising ID in your device settings. Because we do not collect or store personal information on our own servers, requests to know or delete generally concern data processed by our advertising and analytics partner (Google) — see the Google Privacy Policy linked above. Questions: support@gameplaylabs.org.

Children

The app is intended for users 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your choices

Because we store nothing on our side, you can clear all app data at any time by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling it.

Entertainment notice

Astrology and birth chart content is for entertainment purposes only and is not medical, psychological, financial, or legal advice.
