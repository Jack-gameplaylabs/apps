Common questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything you write stays on your device. Nocturne has no account and no server. Uninstalling the app removes your data.

Does Nocturne tell me what my dream means?

No. It offers multiple perspectives and reflective questions so you can find the meaning that fits you. The dictionary is human-curated, not an automated guess.

How are symbols detected in my journal?

Detection happens entirely on your device by matching words against the built-in dictionary. Your dream text is never sent anywhere.
