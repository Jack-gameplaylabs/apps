Support

Need help with Nocturne? We're happy to assist with questions, bugs, and feedback.

Email us at: support@gameplaylabs.org

Please start your subject line with the app name

We support several apps from this address, so please begin your subject with Nocturne — so we can route your message quickly. Example:

  • Nocturne — Bug: insights screen freezes
  • Nocturne — Question: how are symbols detected?
  • Nocturne — Feedback: idea for a new feature

To help us respond faster, include

  • The app version (store listing).
  • Your device model and OS version (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S24, Android 14).
  • A clear description — what happened and what you expected.
  • For bugs: the steps to reproduce, and a screenshot if possible.

Nocturne has no account or login, and stores nothing on our servers, so we never need a password or personal data to help you.
