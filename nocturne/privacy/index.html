Privacy Policy — Nocturne

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Nocturne ("the app", "we") is published by Gameplay Labs LLC. This policy explains what data the app handles. The app has no account, no login, and no server of our own.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers. Your dream entries, your written reflections, tags, and saved symbols are stored only on your device and never leave it (except as needed by the advertising provider below).

Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain information, including device identifiers (such as advertising ID), IP address, approximate location where permitted, and ad-interaction data. This processing is governed by Google's policies. See How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and the Google Privacy Policy.

For users Google identifies as under 18, Google disables ads personalization.

You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads on Android).

Your U.S. state privacy rights

Depending on where you live (for example, California), you may have the right to know, delete, or opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell personal information for money. Under some state laws, showing personalized ads may count as "sharing" or "selling"; you can opt out at any time through the ad privacy options in the app (where available) or by resetting or limiting your advertising ID in your device settings. Because we do not collect or store personal information on our own servers, requests to know or delete generally concern data processed by our advertising partner (Google) — see the Google Privacy Policy linked above. Questions: support@gameplaylabs.org.

Children

Nocturne is intended for adults (18 and older) and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices

Because we store nothing on our side, you can remove all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling it.

Not medical or psychological advice

Dream interpretations and prompts in Nocturne are offered for self-reflection and general interest only. They are not medical, psychological, or mental-health advice. If a dream or your wellbeing is distressing you, please consider speaking with a qualified professional or someone you trust.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@gameplaylabs.org.
