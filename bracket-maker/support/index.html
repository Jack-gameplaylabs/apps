Support

Need help with Tournament Bracket Maker? We’re happy to assist with questions, bugs, and feedback.

Email us at:

support@gameplaylabs.org

Please start your subject line with the app name

We support several apps from this address, so please begin your subject with Tournament Bracket Maker — so we can route your message quickly. Example:

  • Tournament Bracket Maker — Bug: score did not save
  • Tournament Bracket Maker — Question: how do I set up double elimination?
  • Tournament Bracket Maker — Feedback: idea for a new feature

To help us respond faster, include

  • The app version (Settings / store listing).
  • Your device model and OS version (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18).
  • A clear description of the issue or request — what happened and what you expected.
  • For bugs: the steps to reproduce, and a screenshot if possible.

Tournament Bracket Maker has no account or login, and stores nothing on our servers — your brackets stay on your device — so we never need a password or personal data to help you.
